Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the store of the game.

There are four cyrstals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose the game if you total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
